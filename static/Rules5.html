12) Random Events

If configured, random events may occur at the end of any turn with a probability that is configured at the start of the game. Such events might include:

  • "Windy Flip" ... all pieces on the board are flipped
  • "Blue Recall" ... both blue pieces are returned home (blue is just an example)
  • "Yellow Sink Holes" ... all pieces on a yellow hexagon are returned home (yellow is just an example)
  • "Cylinder Power" ... all cylinders gain +1 attacking or defending for that turn (cylinder is just an example)
To disable random events configure a proability of 0.
